MS / Steel Billets

High-quality steel billets manufactured for rolling mills and industrial applications.

Premium-quality Mild Steel (MS) Billets sourced from trusted and reputed steel manufacturers in India. Steel billets are semi-finished steel products that serve as the foundation for manufacturing TMT bars, wire rods, structural steel, and engineering components.

Manufactured using advanced steelmaking processes and strict quality control, our billets offer excellent dimensional accuracy, superior mechanical properties, and consistent chemical composition, making them suitable for domestic and international markets.

TMT Bars

Strong and durable reinforcement bars for construction and infrastructure projects.

Premium-quality Thermo Mechanically Treated (TMT) Bars sourced from leading Indian steel manufacturers. Designed to deliver exceptional strength, durability, and flexibility, our TMT bars are widely used in residential, commercial, industrial, and infrastructure projects worldwide.

Manufactured using advanced Thermo Mechanical Treatment technology, our TMT bars provide superior tensile strength, excellent weldability, and outstanding resistance to earthquakes and corrosion. Every batch is produced under strict quality control to ensure compliance with national and international standards.

Features
